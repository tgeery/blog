Top-bar

This will be identified as a navigation bar with a dark background, containing a button to redirect to the home page and run a search.

<nav class="navbar bg-dark">
 <a class="navbar-brand" style="color:white;" href="/">Home</a>
 <form class="form-inline">
  <input class="form-control" type="search" placeholder="Search">
  <button class="btn btn-outline-success mx-2" type="submit">Search</button>
 </form>
</nav>

Jumbotron

The banner(or jumbotron) at the top will include the title of the page.

<div class="jumbotron" style="margin:0;">
  <h1 style="text-align:center;"<{% block header %}My Blog{% endblock %}</h1>
</div>

Sidebar & Content

The sidebar and content will both sit in the same container, within a row of 12 units split between two columns(2+10).

<div class="contianer-fluid" style="margin:0;">
 <div class="row">
  <div class="col-2" data-spy="scroll" data-offset="0">
   <a class="nav-link" href="/daily">Item 1</a>
   <a class="nav-link" href="/daily">Item 2</a>
  </div>
 </div>
 <div class="col-10 border-left">
  <p class="text-justify">content</p>
 </div>
</div>
